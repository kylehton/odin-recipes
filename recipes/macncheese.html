Mac and Cheese Pasta Recipe

Pot Stirred Macaroni and Cheese

The recipe is for an instant pot mac and cheese. It is the creamiest and most delicious mac and cheese recipe

Ingredients Needed:

  • Macaroni Noodles
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Instant Pot

Steps to Prepare:

  1. Instant cook the noodles, salt, and water on high pressure
  2. Stir the freshly cooked noodles into butter, cheese, and milk until it melts the three added ingredients into a thick sauce
  3. Scoop into a bowl, and serve!
